Domain 3: Commercial, Agentic MMx
Problem
Marketing Mix (MMiX) in pharma is widely used but largely retrospective and manual. Budget decisions are slow, and evidence is scattered (MMM slides, ad-hoc experiments, agency emails). Teams miss in-quarter opportunities because reallocation requires multiple handoffs and re-runs.
Opportunity (What we’ll build)
Create an Agentic MMiX Workflow that:
Reads a predefined dataset with all HCP level promotional activities, sales and demographics data and automatically assigns category of each column (e.g., HCP_ID = HCP Identifier; Month = Time_Stamp; Calls = promotional_activity).
EDA:  Does an EDA for all channels at national and segment level
Reach, Frequency and Engagement summary for all channels at segment level
Overlap of reach between sales and different promotional channels
Overlap of reach between personal and digital channels
Correlation between sales and different promotional channels
Generates GenAI summaries for each step
Ability to export summaries in Excel and PPT
Outlier removal: Automatically removes outlier per pre-defined rules (ability to do outlier removal at national as well as segment level)
Generates GenAI summaries for rational behind outlier removal
Feature Engineering:
Automatically defines transformation (diminishing return: e.g., log) and displays a plot between delta sales and activity with the transformation curve and fit
Ability to combine similar channels (required when multi-collinearity in the system is high)
Generates GenAI summaries rationalizing the functional forms
Model construct: Ability to choose model granularity at entity and time level
Modeling:  Automatically runs incremental model starting with most important channel e.g., calls and keep segment as class variable at each step
Ensure ordinality for important channels (calls, speaker programs, rep email, HQ email)
Ensures good model fit – avoid over fitting
Ensures model robustness by performing cross validation
Ability to go back to outlier removal or feature engineering – if required
Generates GenAI summaries for model performance and rationale
Ability to automatically run GLM, Bayesian, Fixed Effect, Random Effect models for each variation
Ability to score model performance basis: Model Fit, Model Stability and Ordinality, and display top 10 models basis score
GenAI summary for the rationale behind why these models are top models and recommendation for best model
GenAI Critique the solution and use strategies such as Feature transformation, outlier removal and feature removal to ensure the outcome is statistically significant and aligned
Ability to visualize the displayed models
Ability to select and further finetune a model
Ability to display response curves at national and/or segment level for each channel
Ability to export the final model and move to optimization with a click of a button
Mix Optimization (OPTIONAL):
Automatically takes all the response curves and creates 3 scenarios:
Base Case Scenario: No optimization; Use response curves and historical execution
Budget Neutral: Fix spend for each channel to base case and optimize within channel at segment level for maximum profit
Maximum Profit with Current spend: Keep overall spend fixed (but channel level spend can change) and optimize for maximum profit based allocation across channels and segments
Blue Sky scenario: Don’t keep any spend fixed and optimize for maximum profit (mROI =0%)
Ability for user to change constraints basis prompt
Ability for user to create additional scenarios basis prompt
Ability to compare and contrast across scenarios
Visualize all the scenarios
Ability to export selected scenarios in PPT
Goal-based Optimization
NLP based optimization formulation and model trigger (E.g. what strategy can help to further increase the mROI for Email as a channel by 2%)
